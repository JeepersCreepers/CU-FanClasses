Shadow Walker
( Scouts )

Background

Shadow walkers are the stealthiest of the scouts, possessing both concealment and Veil walking. This unique combination lets them get in close to use their otherwise short range. Their origins are shrouded in mystery, but it is said that Shadow Walkers come from an unknown world that conforms neither to Norse cosmology nor what is known of the pre-Piercing world. The first Shadow Walkers learned their abilities in great fear, a tribe hiding in darkness from a powerful enemy. However, that fear became delight, as the ability to walk the shadows proved a distinct advantage.

Motto

The world’s shadows are our home.

Bane Samples

Light Sensitivity

Takes increased damage from radiant and fire abilities.


Incorporeal Fade

Physical attacks deal reduced damage, and metal equipment causes self-damage when activating abilities.

Boon Samples

Moonlight Shadow

Preparation time for entering the Veil is greatly reduced based on concealment.


Can't Outrun A Shadow

Increases movement speed progressively over time while not taking damage, up to a moderate limit.

Component Samples

Fade Into Darkness (Modal)

The user gains increased concealment based on the presence of nearby shadows, but increases the penalties from both Light Sensitivity and Incorporeal Fade.

Animate Shadow (Modal)

Channeling this ability summons and controls a shadowy body which may move a limited range from the user. The user's shadowy body gains greatly increased concealment and movement speed, and may perform any (other) shadow abilities the user posses.

Mimic Blade (Weapon)

A shadowy blade which mimics the weapon wielded by the Shadow Walker. It does no damage, but also does not affect concealment.

Shadow Strike (Style)

Applies a shadow debuff to the target that marks them as sighted for a long duration, greatly reducing their concealment, and applies a shadowy aura only players of the user’s Realm can see. When the user is striking from behind, the mark is undetectable.

Frightful Paranoia (Shout)

The user exits the Veil and draws any nearby enemies within the Veil into the natural world as well. Those enemies have a high amount of panic inflicted on them and are affected by a shadow debuff that greatly reduces their movement speed for a brief duration.

Fear the Dark (Shout)

The user exits the Veil and gains a shadow buff that grants high concealment for a brief duration. Inflicts high panic on nearby enemies, based on the user’s concealment.

Divine Intervention

Ethereal Sabotage

Places a shadow debuff on a deployable object for a long duration. If an enemy approaches and remains within close proximity to the affected object for a brief period of time, this debuff causes damage to the object and any nearby enemies.

Death Curse

The Shadows Know

Applies a shadow debuff to the user's hostile target for a long duration. The debuff causes the target to be marked, while they are within shadow.
