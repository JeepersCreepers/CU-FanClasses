Seer
( Scouts )

Background

The Seers bring scouting to a whole new level with their specialization in Astral projection and remote viewing. However, they need to be careful not to overtax their abilities or be forced to withdraw back into their bodies at an inopportune time.

Motto

Knowledge is power.

Bane Samples

Severed Soul

When an Astral Projection is destroyed it counts as a death for the user. Even though the body is alive, the victim suffers res sickness during which Astral Projection may not be used.


Frail Flesh

While using Astral Projection a Seer does not regenerate blood.

Boon Samples

Silent Soul

An Astral Projection makes no noise


See the Unseen

A Seer can see Veil users within the Veil from outside of it, albeit with reduced visibility.

Component Samples

Astral Projection (Modal)

Creates an Astral Projection of the user which gains high concealment and can use Astral Abilities.

Memorize Location (Astral Ability)

Creates a connection to the current location. Only a limited number of locations may be connected to.

Memorize Enemy (Astral Ability)

Places a magic debuff on an enemy that marks them as sighted for a long duration, and applies a magical aura only players of the user's Realm can see.

Speed of Thought (Astral Ability)

Send the Astral Projection instantly to within a short distance of a chosen Memorized Location or Memorized Enemy.

Divine Intervention

Reveal the Hidden

The Astral Projection and any enemies in a large area loose the benefit of all concealment.

Death Curse

Dissipated Consciousness

When an Astral Projection is destroyed, instead of being thrown back to the body, the user gains a stationary bird's eye view of the area for a long duration. Abilities may continue to be used.
